Privacy Policy

At AquaTrack Daily App, your privacy is our top priority. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal identifiable information (PII) in compliance with GDPR standards.

Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) about you when you download or use the AquaTrack Daily App. There is no requirement to register before downloading the app, and we do not operate any servers that store your PII.

Third-Party Services

The AquaTrack Daily App does not use any third-party advertising platforms, including AdMob or other ad networks. Any data that might be collected by third-party services, such as Google Play Services, is governed by their respective privacy policies and is beyond our control. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information.

PII Collection via Communication

The only instance where we may collect PII is if you voluntarily choose to contact us via email to provide feedback or submit a bug report. In such cases, the personal information we may collect includes your name, email address, and any feedback or survey responses you provide.

How We Use Your PII

Rest assured that any PII we may receive from you will be used exclusively for the purposes of responding to your inquiry, improving our services, and addressing any issues you raise. We will never use your PII for marketing purposes or share it with third parties for commercial gain.

Children's Privacy

Our app, AquaTrack Daily App, is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information (PII) from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected PII from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you believe that we may have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at contact@ingdatasolution.com, and we will take appropriate action.

Your Choice and Control

Providing feedback or reporting bugs is completely voluntary, and you are under no obligation to send us your personal information. Should you have any concerns regarding your privacy or our data practices, or if you require further clarification about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us at contact@ingdatasolution.com. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and thoroughly.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and the updated policy will be posted. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
